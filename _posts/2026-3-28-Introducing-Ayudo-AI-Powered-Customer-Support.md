Customer support is one of the most critical functions for any business, yet it remains one of the most challenging to scale effectively. As AI continues to transform industries, I saw an opportunity to build something that could truly make a difference for support teams worldwide.

## What is Ayudo?

[Ayudo](https://www.ayudo.ai) is an AI-native customer support platform designed to help teams resolve more tickets while reducing burnout. Instead of replacing human agents, Ayudo augments their capabilities by leveraging the latest advances in artificial intelligence.

## Why I Built Ayudo

Having worked extensively in data science and machine learning, I've always been passionate about applying AI to solve real-world problems. Customer support stood out as an area where AI could have an enormous positive impact — both for the businesses serving their customers and for the support agents who often face overwhelming workloads.

## Key Features

**AI-Powered Resolution** — Ayudo uses advanced natural language understanding to automatically resolve common customer queries, freeing up human agents for complex issues that need a personal touch.

**Seamless Integrations** — The platform integrates with popular tools and workflows that support teams already use, making adoption smooth and effortless.

**Built to Reduce Burnout** — We designed Ayudo with agent well-being in mind. By handling repetitive tasks, AI lets support teams focus on meaningful interactions.

## The Vision

At [Ayudo](https://www.ayudo.ai), our mission is simple: **Resolve More. Burn Out Less.** We believe AI should work alongside humans, not replace them, creating a better experience for both customers and support teams.
